## Style Cohesion Notes

To ensure everything reads as a unified Japandi interior:

1. **Wood tone anchor:** Walnut bed (warm, medium) + ash dining table (lighter, natural) + rattan accent (organic). This is intentional variation within one warm-neutral family — not a mismatch.
2. **Fabric palette:** Sofa in oat linen → dining chair seats in ash veneer → bed in walnut → small accessories in clay/cream. One warm neutral family throughout.
3. **Avoid chrome/silver hardware:** The Hamilton's stainless legs are the one exception to manage. Counter with warm accessories (rattan, ceramics, wood).
4. **Texture layering:** Slatted wood (bed) → woven linen (sofa) → smooth veneer (dining) → natural fibre jute (rug). Each piece contributes a different texture in the same palette.
