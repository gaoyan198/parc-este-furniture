Splurge · Master Bedroom
Bed + Bedframe

小红书 · Community Insights

  • Floating / recessed-base platform beds are overwhelmingly recommended on XHS & Lemon8 SG renovation posts — creates visual lightness, makes a 69sqm room feel larger.
  • Walnut veneer and solid oak are the two dominant material choices. Walnut is praised for warm grain that photographs beautifully on home reveal posts.
  • Tatami-style storage beds are a recurring XHS favourite for compact SG condos — eliminates need for bulky storage furniture.
  • Tall tufted headboards are consistently described as "old-fashioned" on XHS and clash with the Japandi wabi-sabi brief. Avoid.

Splurge · Living Room
Sofa
  • Castlery Hamilton is the most-recommended sofa on Singapore Lemon8 for Japandi/Scandi homes — praised for deep seats, pocket spring comfort, removable washable covers.
  • Linen-weave or bouclé in oat, cream, or warm grey is the XHS consensus for tropical climates — breathable, ages beautifully.
  • For small SG living rooms: 2-seater + rattan accent chair reads as "curated" rather than cramped. Adds texture that a 3-seater alone cannot.
  • L-shaped sectionals in sub-800sqft units are consistently regretted on XHS — they dominate the room and block balcony flow.
Room dimension note
The floor plan labels "2.6m" and "2.0m" in the living area — these are likely partial references, not full room dimensions. Measure your actual space before purchasing. The room may be wider. All recommendations below are sized conservatively.

Save · Dining Area
Dining Table + Chairs
  • IKEA LISABO is overwhelmingly the top pick on Singapore Lemon8 renovation accounts for 2BR condo dining on a budget — ash veneer + solid birch, Red Dot Award, legitimately beautiful at the price.
  • For Japandi dining chairs: solid wood chairs without arms (minimalist) or upholstered seats with rounded backs (cosy + practical). IKEA RÖNNINGE appears frequently in Japandi home reveals.
  • Avoid chairs with armrests at compact tables — they take too much width and make squeezing 4 people uncomfortable at a 140cm table.

Japandi Cohesion
Making it all work together
🪵
Wood Tone Anchor
Walnut bed (warm, medium) + ash dining table (lighter) + rattan accent (organic). Intentional variation within one warm-neutral family — not a mismatch.
🧵
Fabric Palette
Sofa in oat linen → dining chairs in ash veneer → bed in walnut → accessories in clay/cream. One warm neutral family throughout.
✋
Texture Layering
Slatted wood (bed) → woven linen (sofa) → smooth veneer (dining) → natural fibre jute (rug). Each piece contributes a different texture within the same palette.
⚠️
Chrome / Silver
The Hamilton's stainless legs are the one exception. Offset with warm accessories — rattan coffee table, ceramic vessels, wood trays — to keep the room feeling Japandi.
