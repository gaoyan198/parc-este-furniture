Why five styles for one unit?

The same 743 sq ft Type BP5 unit at Parc Esta supports very different interior visions. Each style here was researched end-to-end using the same methodology — Xiao Hong Shu (小红书) as the primary source for community-validated picks, supplemented by Singapore Lemon8 home reveal accounts and direct retailer research. Every product listed is real, in stock at the time of writing, and dimensionally checked against the floor plan.

Use the style cards above to browse complete picks for each direction. Switch styles anytime via the top navigation. Hybrid approaches (Japandi bed + Tropical sofa, say) are entirely valid — the cross-style coordination notes inside each page will help.

Research Method

XHS keyword searches in Mandarin, Lemon8 SG home reveals, direct retailer pages (Castlery, BedAndBasics, IKEA, Journey East, Scanteak). Every product page fetched and verified for current SG pricing.

Categories Covered

Master bed + frame (queen, storage-focused), living room sofa, dining table + chairs (4-seater), toilet bowl + bathroom accessories. Three options per category, ranked.

Floor Plan Constraints

Master bedroom ~3.2 × 3.5m, living room ~2.6m wide, L-shaped dining (140cm table max), 1 master bath. Bedroom 2 is a home office — no second bed.
