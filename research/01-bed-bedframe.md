# Research: Master Bed + Bedframe

**Goal:** Queen-size Japandi bed frame, ash/walnut tone, low-profile or platform style.  
**Budget:** Splurge — up to SGD 4,500 (frame only).  
**Room constraint:** Master bedroom ~3.2m wide — queen frame (≤165cm wide) leaves comfortable clearance.

---

## Community Insights (XHS / Lemon8 / Singapore renovation community)

- The **floating / recessed-base platform bed** is overwhelmingly recommended on Singapore Lemon8 and XHS renovation posts for Japandi interiors — it creates visual lightness and makes the room feel larger, critical in a 69sqm unit.
- **Walnut veneer and solid oak** are the two dominant material choices cited. Walnut is praised for its warm grain that photographs beautifully (popular on XHS home reveal posts).
- **Tatami-style storage beds** are a recurring XHS favourite for compact SG condos — the under-bed storage eliminates the need for additional storage furniture, which matters in a 2-bedroom unit where BR2 is a home office.
- The Singapore Lemon8 community frequently recommends Castlery for mid-to-premium beds: "quality feels premium, delivery is smooth, assembly team is professional" (community consensus).
- **Avoid**: tall upholstered headboards with tufting — these are consistently described on XHS as "old-fashioned" and clash with the Japandi wabi-sabi brief.

## Recommendation Summary

**Best overall:** Castlery Joseph Bed Walnut ($1,199) — floating base, walnut veneer, premium warranty, cohesive collection available.  
**Best if you want solid wood:** BedAndBasics Nara American Oak (from $647) — real material, more affordable.  
**Best if storage is priority:** BedAndBasics Take Oak Wood Tatami Storage ($1,169) — built-in drawers, authentic tatami form.

**Mattress note:** Budget separately for a queen mattress — Emma or Sealy are frequently recommended on Singapore Lemon8 for Castlery bed owners.
