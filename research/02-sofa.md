# Research: Living Room Sofa

**Goal:** Compact Japandi sofa in linen/cotton neutral fabric, for a compact living room.  
**Budget:** Splurge — up to SGD 6,000.  
**Room constraint:** Living room ~2.6m wide (per floor plan label). Sofa must be ≤210cm wide. Depth ≤90cm preferred due to ~2.0m room depth.

> **Critical note on room dimensions:** The Parc Esta Type BP5 floor plan labels "2.6m" and "2.0m" in the living room area. These likely indicate partial measurement references rather than full room dimensions. Measure your actual space before purchasing — the room may be wider than 2.6m. However, all options below are sized conservatively to ensure comfort regardless.

---

## Community Insights (XHS / Lemon8 / Singapore renovation community)

- **Castlery Hamilton** is the most-recommended sofa on Singapore Lemon8 for Japandi/Scandi homes — praised for deep seats, pocket spring comfort, and the removable/washable cover system.
- XHS and Lemon8 SG renovation accounts consistently recommend **linen-weave or bouclé fabric in oat, cream, or warm grey** for tropical climates — breathable, ages beautifully, easy to style.
- For small Singapore living rooms, the community consensus is: **2-seater + rattan/wicker accent chair** over a 3-seater — it reads as "curated" rather than cramped, and the accent chair adds texture for Japandi.
- **Avoid**: L-shaped/sectional sofas in compact condos — on XHS, homeowners consistently regret L-shapes in sub-800sqft units as they dominate the room and block flow to balconies.
- **Performance fabric** is heavily cited on Singapore renovation communities because of humidity, perspiration, and spill risk in tropical climates — Castlery's Performance Linen Weave is a popular compromise (looks and feels like linen, but more durable).
- Stainless steel legs are less preferred over **tapered wood legs** for Japandi — wood grounds the aesthetic.

> **Note on Castlery Hamilton legs:** The Hamilton uses stainless steel legs, which is a Japandi aesthetic trade-off. To work around this, many SG homeowners pair the Hamilton with a rattan coffee table and natural-fibre rug to warm up the metal legs.

## Recommendation Summary

**Primary recommendation:** Castlery Hamilton 2-Seater in Performance Linen Weave "Oat" ($1,199) + rattan accent chair (~$300) + jute rug (~$99). Total ~$1,600.  
**If you want more seating:** Upgrade to Hamilton 3-Seater ($1,599) — confirm room width ≥3.0m first.  
**Key shopping tip:** Order fabric swatches from Castlery before committing — they mail them free. Also check if the room has natural light that affects how "oat" vs "cream" reads in your specific unit.
