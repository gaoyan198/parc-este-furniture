# Research: Dining Table + Chairs

**Goal:** 4-seater dining table for an L-shaped dining area off the kitchen. Japandi style, ash or natural oak.  
**Budget:** Save tier — target SGD 400–1,200 for table + 4 chairs combined.  
**Room constraint:** Dining area shares an L-shaped space with the kitchen. 4-seater table at 120–140cm long × 70–80cm wide is ideal.

---

## Community Insights (XHS / Lemon8 / Singapore renovation community)

- **IKEA LISABO** is overwhelmingly the top pick on Singapore Lemon8 renovation accounts for 2BR condo dining on a budget — its ash veneer + solid birch construction is genuine Japandi in character, and the Red Dot Award-winning design gives it a premium look far above its price.
- XHS home reveal posts from Singapore frequently feature extendable dining tables for compact homes — the ability to host 6 when needed is highly valued. **Nook and Cranny Oqui** and **Kave Home Oqui** appear in multiple SG renovation compilations.
- For Japandi dining chairs, the XHS community consistently recommends: **solid wood chairs without arms** (minimalist), or **upholstered seats with rounded backs** (cosy + practical). The IKEA RÖNNINGE (solid birch) appears frequently in Japandi home reveals.
- **Avoid**: chairs with armrests at a small dining table — they take up too much width and make squeezing 4 people around a 140cm table uncomfortable.
- **Material note for Singapore:** Oak and ash veneer hold up well in humidity. Solid rubber wood is also a popular budget-friendly option that ages gracefully.

## Eliminated Options

| Option | Why Eliminated |
|---|---|

| **Extravagant sintered stone tables ($3,000+)** | Over budget for save tier |
| **Dark wenge or espresso dining sets** | Wrong wood tone for ash/walnut neutral palette |
| **Chairs with arms** | Too wide for 4-person seating at a compact table |
| **Plastic/acrylic chairs** | Not Japandi, not cohesive with wood tones elsewhere |

## Recommendation Summary

**Best value (save tier hero):** IKEA LISABO table + 4 chairs set ($439). This is a genuinely beautiful set — ash veneer, Red Dot Award, 4.9 stars. No compromise needed.  
**If you want a darker wood tone:** IKEA MÖRBYLÅNGA table ($699) + RÖNNINGE chairs (×4, ~$240–316). Total ~$940–1,015. Richer colour, pairs beautifully with walnut bed.  
**If you want extendable:** Nook and Cranny Oqui Oval ($1,051 + chairs) — save this budget for when the LISABO needs upgrading in a few years.

**Key tip:** Pair any dining set with linen placemats (Zara Home or IKEA), a rattan tray, and a small ceramic vase on the table. These small accessories cement the Japandi look at minimal cost.
